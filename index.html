Who We Are

We are a team of development driven individuals passionate about constructing beautiful homes for our clients.

Founded in 2007, Feisa Construction Company Limited provides general contracting, construction management and pre-engineered metal building services to a broad range of commercial and industrial clients throughout Kenya.

Initiation

Here the project objective or need is identified. A suitable response to the need is documented in a business case with recommended solution options.When a solution is approved, a project is initiated to implement the approved solution. This is the time when the project team begins to take shape.

Planning

The team identifies all of the work to be done. The projectâ€™s tasks and resource requirements are identified, along with the strategy for producing them. A project plan outlining the activities, tasks, dependencies, and timeframes is created.

Execution

This is the implementation phase, where the project plan is put into motion and the work of the project is performed practically on site. It is essential to maintain control and communicate as needed during each implementation stages. Progress is continuously monitored and appropriate adjustments are made and recorded as variances from the original plan. Status reports are used to highlight the probable end point in terms of cost, schedule, and quality of deliverables. When deliverables have been produced and the customer has agreed on the final solution, the project is ready for closure.

Closure

The importance is providing the final deliverables to the customer, that is:

  • Handing over project documentation to the business
  • Termination of supplier contracts
  • Releasing project resources
  • Communicate the closure of the project to all stakeholders.
